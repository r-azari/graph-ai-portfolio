About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an eight‑week, live online certification offered by The GRAPH Network. It teaches no‑code AI tools that automate data analysis, content creation, knowledge management, and more.

What I’ll Learn

  • Automate documents, spreadsheets, and data tasks.
  • Organize knowledge and conduct literature reviews with AI.
  • Develop custom chatbots and AI workflows.
  • Create professional visuals and presentations.
  • Design a capstone project that solves a real‑world problem.

Quick Facts

  • Duration: 8 weeks (starting May 9 2025)
  • Workshops: Fridays 3 PM GMT or Sundays 5 PM GMT
  • Tuition: $250 per month × 2 (1‑month refund policy)
  • Format: Interactive, live online with hands‑on support
